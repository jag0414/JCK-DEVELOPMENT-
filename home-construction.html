Construction Process

Phase 1: Planning & Permitting

Site evaluation, plan selection or customization, engineering, and permit acquisition. We handle all coordination with municipalities and utility providers.

Phase 2: Foundation & Site Work

Site preparation, foundation excavation, plumbing rough-ins, concrete pouring, and foundation curing. Typically 2-3 weeks.

Phase 3: Framing & Structural

Floor framing, wall framing, roof truss installation, sheathing, and weather barrier application. Typically 3-4 weeks.

Phase 4: Mechanicals & Exterior

HVAC, electrical, and plumbing rough-ins. Exterior finish installation including siding, brick, windows, and roofing. Typically 4-6 weeks.

Phase 5: Interior Finishes

Drywall, interior trim, flooring, cabinetry, countertops, painting, and fixture installation. Typically 6-8 weeks.

Phase 6: Final Details & Walkthrough

Final inspections, punch list completion, cleaning, and comprehensive walkthrough. Certificate of Occupancy obtained.
