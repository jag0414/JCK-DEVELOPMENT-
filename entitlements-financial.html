Development Financing Considerations

Land Acquisition: Typically 20-30% down payment with seller financing or institutional lending for balance. Development contingencies protect buyer during entitlement phase.

Development Costs: Infrastructure development usually financed through construction loans tied to lot takedowns. Interest reserves and contingencies critical for cash flow management.

Home Construction: Vertical construction typically financed on per-unit basis with spec or presale models. Builder relationships and financial strength impact terms.

Exit Strategy: Lot sales to builders, build-to-sell programs, or long-term hold strategies each require different capital structures and partnership arrangements.
